Using Artificial Intelligence to Compose Music

Humans are the only species on Earth to be able to compose, perform, and appreciate music. No other species on Earth has been observed to do all three of those things. Thus, it can be argued that the ability to create music is the quintessential trait that differentiates humans from other species. If we can train an artifical intelligence to compose music, we therefore will have created an algorithm that is able to perform tasks that we believe to be unique to humanity.

Solving a QuizBowl Culture Problem with NodeJS

When the freshmen on my Quiz Bowl team got into some hot water for misbehaving at a meet, I decided it was because of a lack of proper structured practice. Our buzzer sets only allowed for five people on each team which means that most people could not practice at once. I developed a web application that is able to host multiple quiz bowl games and act as a virtual buzzer in order to help solve this problem. You can create servers and play games yourself if you would like.
